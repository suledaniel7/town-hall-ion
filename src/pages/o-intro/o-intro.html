Your Journalists



Your journalists may sign up by registering as formal journalists and applying to your organisation after which you accept their invitation and assign them to a beat. Your Feed is populated with posts from your journalists



Swipe to continue...

Managing Journalists



Journalists are found under the Journalists tab in Town Hall. Slide to the left to manage Journalist Requests and either way to manage accepted journalists.

Direct Messages

You can converse with individual users through their profiles by clicking on the message icon there, while you can view all your conversations by clicking on the message icon on your profile

Posting Content

You may choose to send posts out to all your followers or just those in specific Districts.

Also, appropriately label posts as either News or Opinion for clarity. While your Account may be followed by other accounts, as an Organisation Account, your Account is not designed to follow other accounts.
