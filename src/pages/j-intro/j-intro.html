You can either sign up as a Formal or Freelance Journalist



Formal Journalists' Accounts are managed in part by their Organisations.
Freelance Journalists have complete control over their Accounts



Swipe to continue...

Your Feed



Your Feed is populated by posts from accounts you follow as well as posts on your beat.

Freelance Journalists

Freelance Accounts are unverified by Town Hall. You select a beat to report on and may start reporting right away

Formal Journalists

As a formal journalist, your Account is verified based on the verification status of your organisation.

After creating your Account, you apply to your organisation and await assignment to your beat.

Once you've been assigned, you may begin posting and your posts appear in the Feed of all those following you or users who follow your organisation and are on your beat.

Direct Messages

You can converse with individual users through their profiles by clicking on the message icon there, while you can view all your conversations by clicking on the message icon on your profile

News and Opinion

Classify your posts appropriately as News or Opinion posts to aid clarity
