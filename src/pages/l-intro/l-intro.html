Your Account

As a Legislator, your Account has two metrics: constituents and followers.
All your constituents automatically follow your Account, but are registered as constituents and not followers.
Your followers are non-constituents who choose to follow your Account.

Following



Your Account may be followed by other accounts, but being a Legislator Account is not designed to follow other accounts.

Direct Messages

You can converse with individual users through their profiles by clicking on the message icon there, while you can view all your conversations by clicking on the message icon on your profile

News v. Opinion

To help our users differentiate, Town Hall posts are classified as either News or Opinion.
Being a Legislator Account, all posts from your Account are tagged as News posts.
