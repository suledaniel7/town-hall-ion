Populating Your Feed



Follow Media Organisations to know what is going on with your Legislators.
All posts from their journalists on your districts will show up in your feed. You can also follow other Legislators or Journalists.

Direct Messages

You can converse with individual users through their profiles by clicking on the message icon there, while you can view all your conversations by clicking on the message icon on your profile

News v. Opinion

Posts are classified as News or Opinion to help you understand what is actual fact and what is simply a journalist’s or an organisation’s point of view.
